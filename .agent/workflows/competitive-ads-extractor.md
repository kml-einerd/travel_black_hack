# Competitive Ads Extractor

Extract and analyze competitors' ads from ad libraries.

## When to Use
- Researching competitor ad strategies
- Finding inspiration for your own ads
- Understanding market positioning
- Identifying successful ad patterns
- Analyzing messaging that works

## Process

### 1. Identify Competitors
List main competitors to research.

### 2. Access Ad Libraries
- **Facebook/Meta**: facebook.com/ads/library
- **LinkedIn**: linkedin.com/ad/library (campaign manager)
- **Google Ads**: adstransparency.google.com
- **TikTok**: library.tiktok.com

### 3. Extract Ads
For each competitor, capture:
- Ad creative (image/video)
- Headline
- Body copy
- CTA button text
- Landing page URL
- Ad format
- Run dates (if available)

### 4. Analyze Patterns

**Messaging Analysis:**
- What problems are they highlighting?
- What benefits do they emphasize?
- What emotional triggers are used?
- What CTAs work best?

**Creative Analysis:**
- What visual styles dominate?
- What formats are used most?
- What colors/branding elements?

### 5. Document Findings

```markdown
# Competitor: [Name]

## Top Performing Ads
1. **Ad 1**
   - Headline: [text]
   - Hook: [problem/benefit]
   - CTA: [action]
   - Why it works: [analysis]

## Patterns Identified
- [Pattern 1]
- [Pattern 2]

## Opportunities for Us
- [Gap 1]
- [Gap 2]
```

## Output
- Competitor ad inventory
- Messaging patterns
- Creative inspiration
- Recommendations for your ads
