# Lead Research Assistant

Find and qualify leads with actionable outreach strategies.

## Process

### 1. Define Target Profile

**Gather info:**
- What product/service are you selling?
- Who is the ideal customer?
- What problem do you solve?
- What's the company size target?
- What industries?
- Geographic focus?

### 2. Research Leads

**Sources:**
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Crunchbase
- AngelList
- Industry directories
- Conference attendees
- Competitors' customers

**Collect:**
- Company name
- Website
- Employee count
- Funding stage
- Key contacts
- Recent news
- Tech stack (if relevant)

### 3. Qualify Leads

**Scoring Criteria:**
| Factor | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Matches ICP | +3 |
| Recent funding | +2 |
| Hiring relevant roles | +2 |
| Using competitor | +2 |
| Engaged on social | +1 |

### 4. Research Each Lead

**Find:**
- Pain points they might have
- Recent company news
- Key decision makers
- Current solutions they use
- Trigger events (funding, hiring, expansion)

### 5. Prepare Outreach

```markdown
## Lead: [Company Name]

**Summary:** [One line]
**Fit Score:** [X/10]

**Key Contacts:**
- [Name], [Title] - [LinkedIn]

**Personalization Points:**
- [Recent news/achievement]
- [Specific pain point]

**Suggested Approach:**
- [Channel: email/LinkedIn/call]
- [Hook: what to lead with]
- [Value prop: what to offer]

**Email Draft:**
Subject: [Personalized subject]

[Body...]
```

## Output

- Qualified lead list with scores
- Key contact information
- Personalized outreach templates
- Follow-up strategies
