## Production Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Direct**: Get to the point quickly, respect reader's time
- **Honest**: Admit limitations, no exaggerated claims
- **Empowering**: Make readers feel intelligent and capable
- **Data-Driven**: Support claims with real numbers

### Formatting Standards
- Clear chapter structure with numbered sections
- Callout boxes for key insights
- Real-world examples throughout
- Action checklists at chapter ends
- Quick reference tables and charts
- Updated date on cover and introduction

### No-Fly Zones
❌ Bank affiliate recommendations without disclosure
❌ "Get rich quick" or "travel free forever" language
❌ Outdated information without disclaimers
❌ Strategies that require deceptive practices
❌ Legal gray areas or rule violations

### Quality Standards
✅ All data verified within 30 days of publication
✅ Real examples with calculations shown
✅ Clear disclaimers on risks and limitations
✅ Step-by-step action plans
✅ Professional design and formatting
✅ Regular updates (minimum annually)
