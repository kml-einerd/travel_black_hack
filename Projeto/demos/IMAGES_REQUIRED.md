# Images Required for MilesOS

This document lists the visual assets required to replace the current placeholders in the MilesOS demos. All images should follow the "Fintech Editorial" direction: clean, professional, high-quality lighting (5600K), and avoid generic stock photography.

## Credit Cards
Rendered 3D or high-quality frontal photography on transparent or neutral backgrounds.
- **Chase Sapphire Preferred** (Blue/Metal texture)
- **Amex Gold Card** (Rose Gold texture)
- **Chase Ink Business Preferred**
- **Capital One Venture X**
- **Bilt Mastercard**

## News & Deals
Editorial style photography, 16:9 ratio.
- **Avianca LifeMiles**: Plane tail or stylized logo on neutral background.
- **Business Class Interior**: High-end cabin shot (ANA, United Polaris), focus on seat details/champagne, not people smiling at camera.
- **Hotel Interiors**: Hyatt/Marriott lobby or suite, architectural focus.
- **Mistake Fares**: Abstract map or globe visualization, or destination specific (e.g., Bali landscape for "Bali Mistake Fare").

## Courses & Ebooks
- **Points 101 Cover**: Abstract geometric or typography-led design.
- **Zero to 100k Cover**: Passport/Ticket stylized composition.

## Icons & UI Elements
- **Map Pins**: Custom SVG pins for the Award Map (Green/Blue variants).
- **Category Icons**: Airline tails, Hotel logos (transparent PNGs).
