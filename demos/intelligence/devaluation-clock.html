Devaluation Defense Strategies

1

Never Stockpile Indefinitely

Points are a depreciating asset. The average program devalues 10-15% annually. Earn-and-burn cycles of 12-18 months maximize value.

2

Diversify Your Portfolio

Don't put all eggs in one basket. Spread across Chase UR, Amex MR, and Capital One for maximum flexibility when one program devalues.

3

Book Speculatively

Free cancellation is your friend. When devaluation rumors hit, book now and decide later. Prices only go up.
