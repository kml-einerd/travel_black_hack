DEVALUATION

Amex Platinum: Centurion Lounge Access Restricted

Effective March 1, 2026

American Express is limiting Centurion Lounge access for Platinum cardholders to just 2 visits per year unless you spend $75,000+ annually. Guest access remains at 2 guests when you visit.

Impact: High for frequent Centurion Lounge users who don't meet spend threshold. Consider Delta Reserve or other lounge-access cards as alternatives.
ENHANCEMENT

Chase Sapphire Reserve: New $100 Dining Credit

Effective February 1, 2026

Chase is adding a new annual $100 statement credit for dining purchases, in addition to the existing $300 travel credit. This effectively reduces the net annual fee from $550 to $150.

Impact: Makes the CSR significantly more valuable. With $300 travel + $100 dining credits, the effective fee is just $150/year for premium benefits.
PROGRAM CHANGE

United MileagePlus: Dynamic Pricing Expansion

Rolling out Q1 2026

United is expanding dynamic pricing to all domestic routes. Award prices will fluctuate based on demand. Saver-level awards still available but more limited.

Impact: Medium. Book Saver awards early (330 days out) before prices increase. Consider alternative Star Alliance programs for better value.
